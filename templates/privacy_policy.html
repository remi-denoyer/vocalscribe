About Our App

Vocal Scribe is a bot designed to transcribe vocals into text. Our primary function is to provide transcription services without retaining any personal information or data from our users.

Information Collection and Use

For a better experience while using our service, we may require you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information, including but not limited to your voice recordings. The information that we request will be retained by us and used as described in this privacy policy.

The app does use third party services that may collect information used to identify you. However, Vocal Scribe does not store or retain any personal data or recordings. All transcriptions are processed in real-time, and no data is stored after the transcription process is complete.

This Log Data may include information such as your device Internet Protocol (“IP”) address, device name, operating system version, the configuration of the app when utilizing our Service, the time and date of your use of the Service, and other statistics.
